While dealing with the unbearable agony , she was also angry
about the uncontrolled craziness
about the unleashed hate
and about herself.

"The happiness was delusional and fragile"
she thought
only protected by the ephemeral peace.
She is tired of advocating for the so-called love
over and over again. She is again fearful of the reality; she wants to escape.




Enter The Void.
